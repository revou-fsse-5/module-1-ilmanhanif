About Us

Butterfest is more than just a music festival; it's a celebration of culture, unity, and the transformative power of music. Established in the vibrant and diverse heart of Indonesia, Butterfest brings together artists and music lovers from around the world for an unforgettable experience.

What to Expect

At Butterfest, you can expect:

  • Incredible Lineups: Featuring a mix of established and emerging artists across various genres, including pop, rock, electronic, traditional Indonesian music, and more.
  • Cultural Immersion: Experience Indonesia's rich cultural heritage through traditional performances, local cuisine, and artisanal crafts.
  • Community Engagement: Participate in workshops, panel discussions, and interactive sessions with artists and industry experts.
  • Sustainability Efforts: We're committed to eco-friendly practices, ensuring that Butterfest leaves a positive impact on the environment and local communities.

Join Us

Join us at Butterfest and be part of a festival that celebrates music, culture, and community. Whether you're a passionate music enthusiast, a cultural explorer, or simply looking for an unforgettable experience, Butterfest promises to deliver joy, inspiration, and a sense of unity. Welcome to Butterfest, where the magic of music melts boundaries and brings us together.
